About

Kerma was made by a small group of people. Actually only two people. Andrea Berrocal who came up with the design and also developed the website and Valery Berrocal, who made the illustrations you see on the Kerma website as well as the Kerma Logo. The ultimate goal behind Kerma is to make a decentralized wallet so users can be in charge of their own tokens and not have to worry about others interfering.
